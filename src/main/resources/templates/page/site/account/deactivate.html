Deactivate your account


If you want to temporarily stop using Facebook, you can deactivate your Account. If you want to permanently delete your Facebook Account, let us know.


Deactivate the account This action may be temporary. The system will disable your account and remove your name and photo from most of what you've shared. You can continue to use Messenger.


When you delete your Facebook account, you won't be able to retrieve the content or information you've shared on Facebook. Messenger and all your Messages will also be deleted.
